About Runway

Exploring the Intersection of AI and Color Theory in Graphic Design

Our Mission

At Runway, we're dedicated to unraveling the complex relationship between artificial intelligence and color perception in the realm of graphic design. Our blog serves as a platform for designers, technologists, and curious minds to explore how AI algorithms are reshaping our understanding and application of color theory.

What We Cover

  • AI-driven color palette generation
  • Machine learning in color analysis
  • Perceptual color modelels and AI
  • The impact of Gen-3 Alpha on design workflows
  • Runway ML and its applications in graphic design
